Group Handpan Workshop

A shared sound experience guided by unspokenmelodies

The Handpan Group Workshop is a space where people connect through rhythm, sound, and flow. No experience is needed, only curiosity and your hands. Together, we create an atmosphere of learning, peace, and fun.

Why join group workshop:

  • Meet like-minded people who share the same curiosity for sound and creativity.
  • Laugh, learn, and play together, no pressure, no perfection.
  • Discover how mistakes can turn into music.
  • Experience that "we're all in this together" rhythm.
  • Feel the energy of a group groove and how it changes everything

"It's not just a workshop, it's an experience."

What you will learn:

  • Introduction to the Handpan – posture, touch, and first sounds
  • Experiencing the beauty of group coordination.
  • Learn an emotional melody
  • Basics of improvisation and creating your own melody.
  • Simple Introduction to Music Theory on the Handpan.

Workshop Details:

  • Course Duration: 7.5 hours that be divided into-
    • Three 2.5-hour sessions across a three day time period.
    • N.B; All sessions will be back to back across the set number of days.
  • Format: In-person (small groups, maximum 4 students)
  • Instrument: Provided if you don't own one / bring your own if you have one.
  • Location: Madinaty
